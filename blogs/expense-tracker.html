Expense Tracker App

Overview

Automate your HR approval process with an AI Builder model that can evaluate leave requests and trigger dynamic Power Automate approvals. This approach eliminates manual decisions and ensures faster responses.

Key Steps

  1. Create a SharePoint list for leave requests with columns like Employee Name, Reason, and Manager.
  2. Build a Power Automate flow that triggers on new form submission.
  3. Use AI Builder to analyze request sentiment or extract data from attachments.
  4. Set up conditional approvals — auto-approve based on conditions, or escalate to HR.

Result & Impact

This automation reduces HR load by 70% and ensures consistency across departments. It’s scalable, easy to maintain, and integrates seamlessly with Teams and SharePoint.
